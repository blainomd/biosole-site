The Science

256 pressure sensors.
One clinical picture.

Each insole contains a grid of pressure sensors sampling at 100Hz. Every footstrike generates a plantar pressure map that reveals asymmetry, instability, and deterioration invisible to the naked eye.

footprint

Plantar Pressure Map

Real-time visualization of weight distribution across the foot. Detects forefoot overloading, heel strike asymmetry, and midfoot collapse.

timeline

Temporal Gait Analysis

Stance phase, swing phase, double support time, cadence. Millisecond precision reveals neurological and musculoskeletal patterns.

psychology

AI Pattern Recognition

Claude analyzes pressure trends over weeks. Detects pre-fall gait deterioration 3-4 weeks before a clinical event. Physician attests via ClinicalSwipe.
